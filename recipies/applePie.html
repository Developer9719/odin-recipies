Apple Pie

This is a delicious pie

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 6 to 8 tablespoons ice water
  • 6 to 8 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in ice water, a tablespoon at a time, until dough forms a ball.
  3. Divide dough in half, shape into balls, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Roll out one dough ball on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie pan. Place in the pan and fill with apple slices.
  5. In a bowl, mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Sprinkle over apples. Dot with butter.
  6. Roll out the second dough ball and place over the filling. Seal edges and cut slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Mix egg yolk and milk; brush over the crust.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350째F (175째C) and continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, or until apples are soft and crust is golden brown.
  9. Cool on a wire rack before serving.

Enjoy your homemade apple pie!
